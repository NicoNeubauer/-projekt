What is I-scream?

I-scream is a small and simple ice cream store that sells horror themed ice cream and other products like Sundaes Mousses and Smoothies. I-scream is a small store that's both driven and owned by the local community. Here at I-scream we focus on happy customers and a scary horror menu. For us at I-scream it's also important to use as many organic ,healthy and locally sourced ingredients as possible in our products.
